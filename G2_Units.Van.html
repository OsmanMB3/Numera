Grade 2: Enriching Minds with Mathematics

Number Sense and Operations

  • Develop fluency with addition and subtraction up to 100.
  • Solve problems using mental math, number lines, and fact families.
  • Explore even and odd numbers.
  • Estimate sums and differences.

Patterns and Relationships

  • Identify and extend repeating and growing patterns.
  • Understand inequalities and equations involving addition and subtraction.

Measurement and Geometry

  • Measure using standard metric units (centimeters).
  • Explore 2D and 3D shapes, including sides, vertices, edges, and faces.
  • Compose and decompose simple shapes.
  • Compare lengths, heights, and other attributes.

Data and Probability

  • Interpret and create bar graphs, pictographs, and line plots.
  • Understand and describe the likelihood of events using terms like certain, probable, unlikely, and impossible.
  • Use comparative language to analyze data.

Financial Literacy and Reasoning

  • Recognize coin values and count money up to $1.
  • Solve word problems involving spending and saving.
  • Learn strategies to calculate equivalent coin values and make a dollar.
