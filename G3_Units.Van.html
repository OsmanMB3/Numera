Grade 3: Enriching Minds with Mathematics

Number Sense and Operations

  • Mastery of number concepts up to 1000, including skip counting, place value, addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division concepts.

Fractions

  • Understanding fractions as parts of a whole, on a number line, and in real-world contexts. Emphasis on unit fractions and modeling.

Measurement and Geometry

  • Exploring measurement (length, mass, capacity), area, perimeter, and time concepts, as well as identifying and constructing 2D and 3D shapes.

Patterns and Algebra

  • Understanding and creating increasing, decreasing, and repeating patterns.
  • Solving one-step equations with unknowns and developing pattern rules using words and numbers.

Data, Probability, and Financial Literacy

  • Representing and interpreting data through pictographs, bar graphs, and charts.
  • Understanding likelihood of events; developing fluency with coins and bills up to $100.
