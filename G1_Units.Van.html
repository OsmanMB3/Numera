Grade 1: Enriching Minds with Mathematics

Number Sense and Operations

  • Develop fluency in addition and subtraction up to 20.
  • Understand fact families and relationships between addition and subtraction.
  • Use number lines, cubes, and pictures to model mathematical operations.
  • Solve simple word problems involving addition and subtraction.

Patterns and Relationships

  • Explore repeating patterns with multiple elements (e.g., size, shape, color).
  • Identify, extend, and create patterns.
  • Use patterns to make predictions about sequence.
  • Develop skip-counting skills (by 2s and 5s).

Measurement and Geometry

  • Compare lengths, heights, and sizes using non-standard units (e.g., cubes).
  • Explore 2D and 3D shapes, including counting sides, vertices, edges, and faces.
  • Learn spatial relationships (above/below, beside/next to).
  • Compose simple 2D shapes and explore open vs. closed shapes.

Data and Probability

  • Create and interpret picture graphs, tally charts, and bar graphs.
  • Understand concepts of likelihood (e.g., certain, probable, unlikely, impossible).
  • Use comparative language to describe probabilities (e.g., more likely, less likely).

Financial Literacy and Reasoning

  • Identify coin values (nickels and dimes) and their relationships.
  • Perform simple monetary exchanges using nickels and dimes.
  • Develop reasoning and problem-solving skills through contextualized experiences.
